Athensworks A coworking community in Athens, OH

If you can do your work at a desk or in an open space, we welcome you to check us out. Part of the fun of a coworking space is working with people who do something radically different from you!

Conviently Located

We’re located in uptown Athens, Ohio. Right at the end of Court Street, and you’ll find us not a block later at 29 East Carpenter Street. Look for the glass storefront.

Supportive Community

We are a group of folks working in technology, media, academia, and art.
