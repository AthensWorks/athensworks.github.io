Where are we?

We’re located in uptown Athens, Ohio. Just turn right at the end of Court Street, and you’ll find us not a block later at 29 East Carpenter Street. Look for the glass storefront.

Who work's here

Much of our interest is currently from folks in technology, media, and academia, but we are glad to have as much diversity as we can find seats for.

Who is welcome?

If you can do your work at a desk, we welcome you to check us out. Part of the fun of a coworking space should be working with people who do something radically different from you!
